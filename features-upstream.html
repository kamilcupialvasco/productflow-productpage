From a Vague Idea to a Bulletproof Plan.

Great products aren't built on whims. Our Upstream Workflow provides a structured, repeatable process to take product opportunities from initial analysis to development-ready specifications, with an AI co-pilot to guide you.

The Problem: Chaotic and Inconsistent Discovery

Product discovery is often messy. Good ideas get lost in documents, stakeholder opinions drown out user needs, and teams jump to solutions before the problem is fully understood. This leads to wasted engineering cycles building features that fail to make an impact.

Our Solution: Your Guided Discovery Process

Move opportunities through a clear, customizable Kanban workflow. At each stage, our AI assistant helps you perform the necessary due diligence, from generating research questions and identifying risks to drafting user stories and defining success metrics.

  • ✓Standardize Your Workflow: Guide opportunities through stages like 'Analysing', 'Deep Research', and 'Ready for Discussion'.
  • ✓Leverage an AI Co-Pilot: Let your assistant generate checklists, summarize findings, and identify unknowns.
  • ✓Build Strong Business Cases: Arrive at planning meetings with data-backed, well-researched initiatives.
  • ✓De-risk Ideas Early: Identify and mitigate potential roadblocks before you commit to building.
