Our Methodology

We didn't invent outcome-driven product management, but we did build a platform to make it systematic and scalable. Our tool is built on the shoulders of giants.

Continuous Discovery Habits

Inspired by Teresa Torres

The core of good product management is a deep, ongoing understanding of customer needs. We've built our Feedback Hub and Upstream Workflow around the principles of continuous discovery. We help you map opportunities, generate assumptions, and run experiments, turning discovery into a consistent, repeatable habit for your entire team.

The North Star Framework

Inspired by John Cutler & Amplitude

How do you know if you're successful? The North Star Framework provides a powerful way to connect daily work to long-term value. productflow.online helps you define your North Star Metric and connect every initiative back to the inputs that drive it, ensuring your team stays focused on what truly matters.
