Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes! All new accounts start with a 14-day free trial of our Growth plan, no credit card required. At the end of the trial, you can choose a plan or be automatically moved to our free Starter plan.

What counts as an "insight"?

An insight is any single piece of feedback processed by our system. This could be a support ticket, a tweet, a line from an interview transcript, or an app store review. We count them as they are imported into the Feedback Hub.

Can I change my plan later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time from your account settings. Changes will be prorated.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits or startups?

Yes, we do! Please get in touch with our sales team via the contact page to discuss special pricing for non-profits, educational institutions, and early-stage startups.
