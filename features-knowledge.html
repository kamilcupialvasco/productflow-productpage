Your Team's Central Brain.

Discovery starts long before the first piece of feedback. It starts with a question. Our Knowledge Base is the command center for all your early-stage research, ideation, and problem validation.

Where do good ideas go to die?

In a dozen different documents. In forgotten meeting notes. In a Slack message from three months ago. Without a central, searchable place for insights and research, your team is constantly re-learning the same things and valuable context is lost forever.

Capture, Connect, and Compound Your Knowledge

The Knowledge Base is designed to be the starting point of your discovery process. It's a flexible space to build a durable, interconnected repository of everything your team knows.

  • ✓Capture Raw Insights: Document everything from brainstorming sessions to competitor analysis in a flexible, rich-text editor.
  • ✓Structure Your Research: Turn vague questions into structured research plans with clear hypotheses and methodologies.
  • ✓Connect the Dots: Link insights to customer feedback, discovery interviews, and product initiatives to see the full picture.
  • ✓Make it Searchable: Never lose an insight again. Our powerful search helps you find relevant research from across your entire organization in seconds.
