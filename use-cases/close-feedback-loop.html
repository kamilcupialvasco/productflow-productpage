Close the Feedback Loop.

Track a user's suggestion from the initial idea all the way to release. Know exactly which pieces of feedback were addressed by a new feature launch and show customers you listen.

The Problem: Feedback Goes into a Black Hole

Customers take the time to give you feedback, but they rarely hear back unless it's a support issue. They don't know if their idea was considered, prioritized, or built. This leads to frustrated users who feel ignored, stop offering valuable insights, and are more likely to churn.

Our Solution: End-to-End Traceability

Because productflow.online links feedback to initiatives, you can see exactly which user requests were addressed by a new feature. When you ship, you can easily generate a list of users to notify, turning a product release into a powerful customer engagement and retention tool. It shows your users that their voice matters and that you are actively building the product for them.
