Standardize Your Discovery Process.

Bring order to the chaos. Our upstream Kanban workflow provides a repeatable process for validating ideas before a single line of code is written.

The Problem: Discovery is a "Black Box"

For many teams, discovery is an unstructured, inconsistent process. Some ideas get rigorous research, others are fast-tracked based on a hunch. This leads to an uneven quality of initiatives hitting the roadmap, and a lot of wasted effort on features that haven't been properly de-risked.

Our Solution: A Guided, Visible Workflow

The Upstream Workflow provides a clear, visible path for every opportunity. Customize your stages—like 'Analysing', 'Deep Research', 'Solutioning', 'Ready for Discussion'—and ensure every idea goes through the same validation process. The AI Co-pilot helps ensure no steps are skipped.
