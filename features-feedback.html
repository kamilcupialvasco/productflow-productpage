Turn Customer Noise into Product Signal.

Your users are telling you what to build. Are you listening? productflow.online brings every piece of feedback—from support tickets to app store reviews—into one intelligent, actionable hub.

The Problem: Feedback is Everywhere and Nowhere

Valuable user feedback is scattered across a dozen different channels: Zendesk, Slack, Twitter, sales calls, app reviews. It's a full-time job just to keep track of it all. Important insights get lost, trends are missed, and product decisions are made in a vacuum.

Bring Everything Together

Connect all your data sources in minutes. Our powerful integrations pull feedback from the tools you already use, creating a single, searchable repository for all qualitative user data. Stop the endless copy-pasting.

  • ✓ Integrates with Zendesk, Jira, Slack, Intercom, App Stores, and more.
  • ✓ Use our API or Zapier for custom data sources.
  • ✓ Never lose an important piece of feedback again.

Find the Signal in the Noise

Let our AI do the heavy lifting. We automatically cluster similar feedback, analyze sentiment, and generate concise problem descriptions so your team can focus on solving problems, not sorting spreadsheets. Identify trends and surface critical issues before they escalate.

  • ✓ AI-powered clustering and anomaly detection.
  • ✓ Automatic sentiment and keyword analysis.
  • ✓ Filter and segment feedback by user property (e.g., plan, company size).

  • ✓ Quantify opportunities by seeing how many users are affected.
  • ✓ Close the loop by tracking an insight all the way to release.
