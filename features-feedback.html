Features / Feedback Hub

Turn Customer Noise into Product Signal.

Stop juggling spreadsheets and Slack channels. Aggregate user insights from any source, analyze them with AI, and connect them directly to your product initiatives.

Is Your Feedback a Mess?

Valuable user feedback is scattered everywhere: support tickets, sales calls, app store reviews, Twitter, and a dozen other places. Without a central hub, important insights get lost, trends are missed, and you end up prioritizing based on the loudest voice, not the most critical need.

Our Solution: Your Central Insights Hub

The Feedback Hub is your single source of truth for what your users are thinking and feeling. Connect all your feedback channels and let our AI get to work.

  • ✓Aggregate Everything: Pull in feedback from Zendesk, Intercom, App Stores, Slack, Zapier, and more.
  • ✓Find Signal with AI: Automatically cluster similar feedback, analyze sentiment, and generate problem statements.
  • ✓Segment Your Insights: Filter feedback by user segment, company size, plan type, or any custom attribute.
  • ✓Make it Actionable: Link insights directly to product opportunities and initiatives with one click.

> 🔍 AI Summary of Feedback Cluster #14

Problem: Users find the mobile export process confusing and slow.

Sentiment: Negative (-0.8)

Sources: App Store (12), Zendesk (8), Twitter (5)

Key Quote: "It took me five taps to export a simple report, I almost gave up."

Action: Link to new "Improve Mobile Export" initiative

Who Is This For?

Product Managers

Stop relying on anecdotal evidence. Quantify user needs, identify the most impactful problems to solve, and build a data-driven case for your roadmap priorities.

UX Researchers

Centralize all your qualitative data in one place. Use AI to surface themes and trends across interviews, surveys, and usability tests, and make your research actionable.
